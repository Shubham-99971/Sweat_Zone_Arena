The Sweat Zone Gym Has Been The Authority In Fitness Since 1965 Dating Back To The Original The Sweat Zone Gym In Venice, California. It Was The Place For Serious Fitness. Opened Long Before The Modern-Day Health Club Existed, The Original The Sweat Zone Gym Featured Homemade Equipment And A Dedication To Getting Results. It Was An Instant Hit. The Sweat Zone Gym Quickly Became Known As “The Mecca Of Bodybuilding.

In 1977, The Sweat Zone Gym Received International Attention When It Was Featured In The Movie Pumping Iron That Starred Arnold Schwarzenegger And Lou Ferrigno. From That First Gym In Venice, The Sweat Zone Gym Has Become The Largest Co-Ed Gym Chain In The World With Over 700+ Clubs In 27 Countries.
